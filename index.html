About Us

Established in 1994, Maira Montaj , is a wholly owned Romanian private company operating in the electrical field. Maira Montaj focuses on both the manufacturing and the marketing of electrical equipment. The company works on a contract basis and provides 24/7 service, periodic revisions and repairs for transformer stations and electrical installations, grounding checks, thermal protection for office building, production halls and commercial spaces in Bucharest as well as other locations in Romania. Our portfolio also includes repairs and production of spare parts for oleo pneumatic mechanisms, high voltage circuit breakers with oil and SF 6 extinguishing medium, measuring transformers and switches parameters.

Maira Montaj has specialized staff with a solid professional training and a wealth of experience. We are a group that constantly attempts to generate constructive ideas, continually interacting and pursuing the full satisfaction of our clients’ requirements, through quality services rendered for their benefit

In order to improve its own work and the quality of its services, Maira Montaj has implemented and maintains an integrated quality environment system in compliance with the requirements of SR EN ISO 9001/2015 and SR EN ISO 14001/2015.
